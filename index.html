WELCOME TO GL.BAJAJ

About

G.L. Bajaj Institute of Technology & Management is a prestigious Institute established in the year 2007. It is affiliated to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University and has been approved by AICTE. The Institute currently offers various courses including B.Tech at the UG level and M. Tech, MBA, and MCA at the PG level.

The Institute has maintained a good placement record till now. In 2021, 240 Recruiters took part in the placement drive and the highest package went up to 31.6 LPA. Some of the names of top recruiters who had visited the campus are TCS, Amazon, Capgemini, Accenture, BOSCH and many more.

Event

GL Bajaj Educational Institutions took part in one of the biggest Educational Expo ‘India International Education Expo 2023’ (IIEE 2023) from 28th April to 30th April 2023.

With enormous footfall of students from various parts of the country, the three-day expo included glimpses of GL Bajaj programs, highlights of the success stories of GLBians, the capstone projects taken up by the students and other milestones. The major attraction were the robots and robotic kits produced by the students.

Dr. Mahesh Sharma, Member of Parliament, Gautam Buddh Nagar graced the GL Bajaj’s stall and praised the work done by the Institution on the various fronts. With three days of high action, meeting numerous potential students and visitors, it was a great experience of participation at Education Expo 2023 with a great response.

Excellence Awards 2023

India International Educational Excellence Awards awarded GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management (GLBITM) with the “Best Institute in Technology and Management in North India” and GL Bajaj Institute of Management (GLBIM), Greater Noida with the “Best Private Higher Education Institute in India” for UG courses.

The award was given by Dr. Mahesh Sharma, Member of Parliament, Gautam Buddh Nagar, and Dr. Manas Kumar Mishra, Director, GLBITM, Prof. (Dr.) Mukul Gupta, Principal, GLBIM received the awards with colleagues on behalf of the Institutions.

GL Bajaj Educational Institutions took part in one of the biggest Educational Expo ‘India International Education Expo 2023’ (IIEE 2023) from 28th April to 30th April 2023. This award is an honor for the college, its faculty member, students, and all stakeholders involved in making GLBITM and GLBIM, the Best amongst others.
